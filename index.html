The Truth Engine Difference

Why Our Data Doesn't Lie

We go beyond "wheelchair accessible" labels. Every measurement is verified, every detail matters.

Granular Measurements

Not just "accessible." We measure ramp gradients in degrees, door widths in centimeters, and clearance spaces.

  • Ramp angles measured with inclinometer
  • Door widths verified with tape measure
  • Bathroom layouts photographed & measured

Community-Verified

Trained volunteers and disability advocates conduct real audits. No bots, no assumptions.

  • Audits by people with lived experience
  • Multiple verification sources per location
  • Regular updates from the community

Civic Action Powered

Your reports trigger real municipal action. We track responses and resolutions.

  • Direct reporting to Udaipur Municipal Corporation
  • Public tracking of issue resolution
  • Monthly impact reports published
